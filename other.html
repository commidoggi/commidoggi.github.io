Audio Equipment and Records

I inherited this old JVC stero system from my grandparents, and it has been a great starter setup. It didn't come with a turntable, but I bought an Audio Technica LP60x to go with it. I'm hoping to upgrade soon, and I have an extra Akai stereo I found at a garage sale that has yet to be tested. It needs new belts for the cassette deck and turntable, but it should be finished soon. A few of my favorite records are Loveless - My Bloody Valentine, Little Dark Age - MGMT, and Wildflower - The Avalanches.
